Endangered polar bear because of melt of glacier.

you might have seen this picture before. there are not enough glacier for them to step on. They can swin but they can't live without ground. A swift melt of glacier made their environment harsh to live. Actually the lack of glacier means that they must swim at least 60~70 miles though they can swim until 100miles depending on bear. increasing distance they have to swim signifies a probability for them to be drowned from exhaustion and hypothermia . Unfortunately, the number of drowned bear has increased gradually.
