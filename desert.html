Q: Have you ever thought about snowing desert?

According to Reuter, on Jan 7th 2018, it snowed in the Sahara Desert, one of the hottest regions in the world. and the precipitation was a matter of 16 inches and it looked like covered by white blanket. According to researchers, this phenomenon is not so rare thing. However, increasing frequency threatens scientists who are anxious about disaster by climate change and it is perceived as an evil sign.
